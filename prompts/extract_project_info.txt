You are an architecture news analyst. Extract project information from the article summary below.

IMPORTANT: Do NOT use any emoji in your response.

=== ARTICLE ===
Summary: {summary}
Title: {title}
Source: {source_name}
URL: {url}

=== EXTRACTION RULES ===

1. Only extract information that is EXPLICITLY mentioned in the summary
2. Use null for any field that is not clearly stated
3. For news articles, interviews, or opinion pieces NOT about a specific building, return is_project: false

=== PROJECT TYPES ===
- residential: Houses, apartments, residential towers
- commercial: Offices, retail, corporate headquarters
- cultural: Museums, galleries, theaters, concert halls
- educational: Schools, universities, libraries
- healthcare: Hospitals, clinics, medical centers
- hospitality: Hotels, resorts, restaurants
- mixed_use: Combined residential/commercial/other
- infrastructure: Bridges, stations, airports, utilities
- landscape: Parks, plazas, urban design, public spaces
- interior: Interior design projects only
- masterplan: Urban masterplans, campus plans
- installation: Pavilions, temporary structures, art installations
- other: Anything that doesn't fit above categories

=== PROJECT STATUS ===
- completed: Built and finished
- under_construction: Currently being built
- announced: Plans revealed, not yet started
- competition: Design competition entry or winner
- renovation: Restoration or adaptive reuse
- demolished: Article about demolished building

=== OUTPUT FORMAT ===

Return ONLY valid JSON:

{{
    "is_project": true/false,
    "project_name": "Name of the building/project or null",
    "architect": "Architect/firm name or null",
    "location": {{
        "city": "City name or null",
        "country": "Country name or null"
    }},
    "project_type": "type from list above or null",
    "project_status": "status from list above or null",
    "confidence": 0.0-1.0
}}

Summary: "BIG has completed the Spiral, a 66-story office tower in Hudson Yards, New York."
